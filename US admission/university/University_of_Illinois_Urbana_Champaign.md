# University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign

## Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A bachelor's degree equivalent to that granted by the University of Illinois. A three-year undergraduate degree may not be eligible.
- **GPA:** A minimum GPA of 3.0/4.0 in the last two years of undergraduate study is required. For the Master of Computer Science (MCS) program, a 3.2 GPA or higher is recommended.
- **Prerequisites:** A strong background in computer science is required, including:
  - Computer programming (C++ or Java)
  - Data structures and algorithms
  - Computer architecture
  - Theory of computation

### Application Materials
- A completed online application with a fee.
- A current resume.
- Transcripts from all post-secondary institutions.
- A personal statement.
- An academic statement of purpose.
- Letters of reference.

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:**
  - TOEFL, IELTS, or Duolingo scores are required for non-native English speakers.
  - **Important:** A TOEFL iBT speaking score below 22 significantly lowers the chance of admission and funding.
  - An exemption may be granted if you have completed at least two years of full-time study in a country where English is the primary language.
