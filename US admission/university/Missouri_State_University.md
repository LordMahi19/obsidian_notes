# Missouri State University

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Program Duration:** 2 years (30 credit hours).
- **Application Fee:** A non-refundable application fee is required.
- **GRE:** May be required for some applicants. The reported average score for admitted students is 296.

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A U.S. bachelor's degree or its equivalent.
- **GPA:** A minimum GPA of 2.75 (on a 4.0 scale) in the last 60 credit hours of study.

### Application Deadlines
- **Spring Admission:** November 1
- **Fall Admission:** Please refer to the university website for the most current deadline.

### International Student Requirements
- **Financial Documentation:** Proof of sufficient financial resources is required for visa purposes.
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 80
  - **IELTS:** 6.5
  - **PTE:** 59
  - **Duolingo:** 115

### Financial Aid
- International students are eligible for merit-based "MSU Foundation Scholarships."
