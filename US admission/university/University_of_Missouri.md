# University of Missouri (Mizzou)

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Application Fee:** An application fee is required (e.g., the online program fee is $100).
- **GRE:** An official GRE score report is required for the on-campus program.

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A bachelor's degree from an accredited institution.
- **GPA:** A minimum GPA of 3.0 in the last 60 hours of undergraduate coursework.

### Application Materials
- A completed online application.
- Unofficial transcripts (official transcripts are required upon acceptance).
- Three letters of recommendation (at least one from an academic source).
- A Statement of Purpose.
- A current Resume/CV.

### Application Deadlines
- **Fall Admission:** March 1
- **Spring Admission:** October 1
*(Note: These are the deadlines for the online program and are likely similar for the on-campus program. Please verify on the department website.)*

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 80
  - **IELTS:** 6.5
  - **PTE:** 59
  - **Duolingo:** 115
  - **Cambridge C1 Advanced:** 180
