# Northwest Missouri State University

## Admission Requirements

### Program Details
- **Program Name:** Master of Science in Applied Computer Science (MSACS).
- **Program Duration:** 16 months.
- **STEM OPT Extension:** This program qualifies for the 24-month STEM OPT extension.

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A four-year undergraduate degree from an accredited institution.
- **GPA:** A minimum undergraduate GPA of 2.75 is required, but 3.0 is preferred.
- **Prerequisites:**
  - A major or minor in a technical computing discipline.
  - **Mandatory:** Successful completion of a data structures course is required for admission.
  - Coursework in database systems, discrete mathematics, and statistics is also required.

### Standardized Tests
- **GRE:** The GRE is optional, but submitting scores is recommended for applicants with a GPA below 3.0. A score of 290 or higher is considered beneficial.

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 71
  - **IELTS:** 6.0
  - **Duolingo:** 105

### Financial Aid
- **CS/IS International Graduate Achievement Scholarship:** A one-time $500 scholarship is automatically awarded to eligible international students for their first semester.
