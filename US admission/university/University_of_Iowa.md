# University of Iowa

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Application Fee:** $100 for international applicants.
- **GRE:** The GRE General Test is optional for all applicants.

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A four-year undergraduate degree or its equivalent.
- **GPA:** A minimum undergraduate GPA of 3.00 on a 4.0 scale is required. (Note: Successful applicants often have a higher GPA).
- **Prerequisites:** A strong background is expected in core computer science areas, including:
  - Mathematics (calculus, discrete mathematics, probability)
  - Programming (C, C++, Java) and language foundations
  - Data structures and algorithms
  - Software and hardware systems

### Application Materials
- A completed online application.
- Three letters of recommendation.
- A statement of purpose.
- A curriculum vitae (CV) or résumé.

### Application Deadlines
- **Fall Admission:** January 1
- **Spring/Summer Admission:** Rarely offered; contact the department before applying.

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:**
  - **TOEFL iBT:** A minimum score of 81 is required by the Graduate College, but the CS department generally does not admit students with scores below 100.
  - **IELTS:** A minimum score of 7.0 is required, with no subscore below 6.0.
  - **PTE Academic:** A minimum score of 50.
  - **Duolingo:** A minimum score of 100.
