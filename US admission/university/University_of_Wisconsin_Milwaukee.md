# University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **GRE:** Recommended but not required. A quantitative score of 155 or better is desired by the faculty.

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related discipline.
- **GPA:** A minimum undergraduate GPA of 2.75 on a 4.0 scale. (Conditional admission may be possible for applicants with a GPA between 2.50-2.74 if they meet other criteria).
- **Prerequisites:**
  - Two programming courses.
  - At least 6 additional credits of coursework in Computer Science.
  - One calculus course.

### Application Deadlines (MS in Computer Science)
- **Fall Admission:** March 15
- **Spring Admission:** September 15

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** Submission of TOEFL, IELTS, PTE, or Duolingo scores is required for non-native English speakers.
