# Northeastern Illinois University

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Application Fee:** $30.

### Application Deadlines (International)
- **Fall Admission:** June 15
- **Spring Admission:** October 1
- **Summer Admission:** March 1

### Application Materials
- A completed online application.
- Official transcripts from all colleges and universities attended.
- Two letters of recommendation.
- A statement of goals and objectives.

### International Student Requirements
- **Transcript Evaluation:** For institutions outside the U.S., transcripts must be evaluated by a NACES member company.
- **Financial Documentation:** Proof of sufficient funds to cover the first year of expenses is required.
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 79
  - **IELTS:** 6.5
  - **PTE Academic:** 53
  - **Duolingo:** 120
