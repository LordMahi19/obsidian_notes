# Concordia University-Chicago

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Program Duration:** 2 years.
- **Application Fee:** A non-refundable application fee is required (please verify the exact amount on the university website).

### Academic Requirements
- **GPA:** A cumulative GPA of 2.85 or above on a 4.0 scale.
- **Prerequisites:** Applicants should have a background in Computer Science, including:
  - C++ or Java
  - Data Structures
  - Operating Systems
  - Computer Architecture
- Students who do not meet all prerequisites may be required to take placement exams.

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** A minimum TOEFL score of 72 (iBT) or an equivalent score from IELTS or another accepted test.
- **Degree Equivalency:** A "3+2" Master's degree is considered equivalent to a 4-year Bachelor's degree for admission purposes.

### Standardized Tests (GRE/GMAT)
- **Test-Optional:** The program has a test-optional policy. However, submitting GRE or GMAT scores is strongly recommended for applicants who do not have a strong academic background in computer science or mathematics.
