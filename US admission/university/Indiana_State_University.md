# Indiana State University

## Admission Requirements

### Key Requirement: Technical Interview
- All applicants must complete a technical interview via Skype, Teams, or Zoom.
- The interview assesses:
  - Clear communication in English.
  - Basic programming skills.
  - Fundamental understanding of data structures and algorithms.

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A 4-year undergraduate or bachelor's degree.
- **GPA:** A minimum cumulative GPA of 2.7 is required, with a 3.0 GPA in the last 60 undergraduate hours and in the major field of study.

### Application Materials
- A completed online graduate admission application.
- Official transcripts from all previously attended institutions.
- A current resume.

### Application Deadlines
- **Fall Admission:** July 15
- **Spring Admission:** November 15

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** Official scores from an approved English proficiency test are required.
- **Transcript Evaluation:** An external evaluation of all international transcripts is mandatory.
- **Orientation:** International students must arrive on campus at least one week before classes begin for a mandatory orientation.

### Financial Assistance
- **Assistantships:** Graduate assistantships, which include a tuition waiver and a stipend, are available for applicants who meet higher admission standards.
