# University of Central Arkansas

## Admission Requirements

**Note:** The following are general requirements for international graduate admission. Please consult the Computer Science department's website for additional, program-specific requirements and deadlines.

### Academic Requirements
- **GPA:** A minimum GPA of 2.70 on a 4.0 scale is required, though some programs may require a higher GPA.
- **Transcripts:** Non-U.S. transcripts must be evaluated by a NACES-approved company (e.g., IEE, Span Tran, ECE), including a course-by-course analysis.

### Standardized Tests
- **GRE:** Generally not required for graduate admission.

### International Student Requirements
- **Financial Documentation:** A Financial Support Statement and proof of sufficient funds are required before an I-20 can be issued.
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 79
  - **IELTS:** 6.5

### Financial Aid
- **Tuition Waiver:** International students who live on campus may be eligible for an out-of-state tuition waiver.
