# Northern Illinois University

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Application Fee:** $60 USD.
- **GRE:** The Standard GRE Examination is required.

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** An undergraduate degree in Computer Science or a closely related field.
- **GPA:** A minimum GPA of 2.75 on a 4.0 scale.

### Application Materials
- A completed online application.
- Official undergraduate transcripts.
- Two letters of recommendation (preferably from academic sources).
- A Statement of Purpose.
- A current Resume or CV.

### Application Deadlines (International)
- **Fall Admission:** May 1st
- **Spring Admission:** October 1st

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** Submission of TOEFL, IELTS, PTE, or Duolingo scores is required.

### Financial Aid
- Funding is available through competitive graduate assistantships and general university scholarships.
