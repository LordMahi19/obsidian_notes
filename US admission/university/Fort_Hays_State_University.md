# Fort Hays State University

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Application Fee:** $50 (non-refundable).

### Application Deadlines (International)
- **Fall Semester:** June 1st
- **Spring Semester:** October 1st

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A bachelor's degree from an accredited institution.
- **GPA:** A minimum GPA of 3.0 in the last 60 undergraduate hours.
- **Transcripts:** For degrees from outside the U.S. or Canada, an official NACES-approved course-by-course transcript evaluation is required.

### Program-Specific Requirements
- Two letters of recommendation.
- A personal statement discussing your reasons for seeking this degree.
- A current résumé.
- Prerequisite courses may be required depending on your academic background.

### International Student Requirements
- **Financial Statement:** An original, certified bank statement showing the ability to cover at least one year of academic costs.
- **Passport:** A copy of your passport ID page.
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 79
  - **IELTS Academic:** 6.5
  - **Duolingo:** 110
  - **iTEP:** 3.9
