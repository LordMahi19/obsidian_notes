# Loyola University Chicago

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Admission Policy:** The university has a rolling admission policy, but applying early is recommended.
- **Prerequisites:** Applicants can be accepted without having all undergraduate prerequisites in computer science.

### Application Materials
- A completed online application.
- Official transcripts from all post-secondary institutions (with English translations if applicable).
- Letters of recommendation.
- A personal statement.
- A current resume or CV.

### International Student Requirements
- **Financial Documentation:** A completed Declaration and Certification of Finances form.
- **Passport:** A copy of your passport.
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL:** 79
  - **IELTS:** 6.5
  - **PTE:** 53
  - **Duolingo (DET):** 110
