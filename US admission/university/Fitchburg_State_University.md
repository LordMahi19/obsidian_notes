# Fitchburg State University

## Admission Requirements

### Program Details
- **F-1 Visa Eligibility:** This is one of only two graduate programs at the university eligible for F-1 visa-seeking international students.
- **Modality:** The program is available both on-campus and 100% online.

### Application Deadlines (International)
- **Fall Admission:** May 1st
- **Spring Admission (from outside the U.S.):** November 1st
- **Spring Admission (transferring within the U.S.):** December 1st

### Application Materials
- A completed application.
- Official college transcripts, which must include a course-by-course evaluation.
- A current resume.
- A personal statement.
- Three letters of recommendation.
- A copy of your passport.

### Academic Requirements
- A bachelor's degree from a regionally accredited institution (or a 4-year equivalent).
- While no specific major is required, preference is given to applicants with backgrounds in computer science, mathematics, engineering, or related fields.

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 79
  - **IELTS:** 6.5
  - **Duolingo:** 105
  - **PTE Academic:** 58
