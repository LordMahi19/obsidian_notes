# Concordia University-Wisconsin

## Admission Requirements

### Program Details
- **Concentrations:** The Master of Science in Computer Science (MSCS) program offers concentrations in Software Engineering and Information Systems.

### Academic Requirements
- **GPA:** A cumulative GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale is required for full admission.
- **Conditional Admission:** Applicants with a GPA between 2.25 and 2.99 may be considered for conditional admission.

### Application Materials
- Completed online application.
- Official transcripts from all post-secondary institutions.
- A current résumé.
- A one-page essay detailing your reasons for pursuing the degree, career goals, and why you chose Concordia University.
- Two letters of recommendation.
- A brief video introduction.
- A copy of a government-issued ID.

### International Student Requirements
- **Financials:** Proof of financial support and a copy of your passport.
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for full admission:
  - **IELTS:** 6.5
  - **TOEFL:** 79
  - **PTE:** 55
  - **Duolingo:** 90
