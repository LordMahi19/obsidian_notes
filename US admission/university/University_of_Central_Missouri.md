# University of Central Missouri

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Application Fee:** $75 (non-refundable).
- **GRE/GMAT:** May be required for some applicants. A GRE score of 290 or higher is recommended if submitting.

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** An awarded bachelor's degree from an accredited four-year institution or its international equivalent.
- **GPA:** A minimum undergraduate GPA of 2.8 is required for the Computer Science program.

### Application Materials
- A completed online application.
- Official transcripts of all undergraduate and graduate coursework.
- A Statement of Purpose.
- A current Resume/CV.
- Letters of Recommendation (if required by the program).

### Application Deadlines (International)
- **Fall Admission:** June 1
- **Spring Admission:** October 1
- **Summer Admission:** March 1

### International Student Requirements
- **Passport:** A copy of your passport.
- **Financial Documentation:** An official bank statement and a declaration of financial support.
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 79
  - **IELTS:** 6.0
  - **Duolingo:** 120
