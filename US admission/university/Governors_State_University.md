# Governors State University

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Program Duration:** 24 months.
- **Application Fee:** $50 (non-refundable).

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** An acceptable bachelor's degree from an accredited institution.
- **Credential Evaluation:** For degrees obtained outside the U.S., a NACES-approved course-by-course credential evaluation is required.

### International Student Requirements
- **Financial Support:** Evidence of financial support to cover all expenses for the duration of the program is required.
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **IELTS:** 6.0 (Academic version).
  - **TOEFL iBT & PTE:** Please refer to the official GSU Catalog for required scores.

### Other Potential Requirements
The following documents may also be required. Please verify with the specific program department.
- Resume
- Personal Statement
- Letters of Recommendation
- GRE/GMAT scores
