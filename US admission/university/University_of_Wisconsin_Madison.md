# University of Wisconsin-Madison

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **GRE:** GRE scores are not required for admission.

### Academic Requirements
- **GPA:** A minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale is generally expected.
- **Prerequisites:** A strong background is required, including:
  - Programming experience (Java and Python are primarily used).
  - Coursework in data structures and machine organization.
  - At least one year of college-level mathematics (calculus level or above).

### Application Materials
- A completed online application.
- A statement of purpose.
- A current CV/resume.
- Three letters of recommendation.
- Unofficial transcripts.

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** If English is not your native language, you must submit proficiency scores. Minimums are:
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 92
  - **IELTS:** 7.0
  - **Duolingo:** 120

### Professional Master's Program (PMP)
- For students without a CS bachelor's degree, a Professional Master's Program (PMP) is available.
- This program only admits for the fall term, with an application deadline typically around February 15.
