# Bradley University

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Program Duration:** 1.5 to 2 years (18-24 months).
- **Application Fee:** $50 USD.

### Application Deadlines (International)
- **Fall Semester:** June 15
- **Spring Semester:** August 15

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** Must hold the equivalent of a U.S. Bachelor's degree. A three-year degree is generally not sufficient.
- **GPA:** A minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale in the last 60 hours of undergraduate coursework.

### Prerequisite Coursework
A strong background in computer science is required, including:
- Discrete mathematics
- At least two semesters of calculus, matrix, or linear algebra
- At least one semester of calculus-based statistics
- A minimum of 15 hours of computer science coursework, covering topics like:
  - Structured or object-oriented programming
  - Elementary and advanced data structures
  - Assembly language
  - Introductory computer architecture

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** Submission of TOEFL, IELTS, or PTE scores is required.
