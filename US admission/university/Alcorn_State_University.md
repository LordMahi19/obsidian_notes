# Alcorn State University

## Admission Requirements

### General Requirements
- **Online Application:** A completed online application is required.
- **Letters of Recommendation:** Two letters of recommendation must be submitted.
- **Official Transcripts:** Official transcripts from all previously attended institutions are necessary.
- **GPA:** A minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 in a bachelor's degree is required.
- **GRE Scores:** Official GRE General Test scores, not older than five years, must be submitted. The institution code is 4008.
- **Immunization Record:** Proof of immunization is required.

### International Student Requirements
- **Credential Evaluation:** A credential evaluation from an approved service is mandatory.
- **Medical Tests:** A Tuberculosis Chest X-Ray and Blood Test are required.
- **Statement of Finances:** A statement of financial support must be provided.
- **English Proficiency:** Official TOEFL or IELTS scores are required to demonstrate English proficiency.

### Program Details
- **Credit Hours:** The program requires 33 credit hours to complete.
- **Tracks:** The program offers thesis, non-thesis, and internship tracks for degree completion.
