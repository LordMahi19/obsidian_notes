# Southern Illinois University Edwardsville

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Application Fee:** $40.
- **GRE:** Recommended but not required.

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A bachelor's degree from an accredited institution. An undergraduate major in science, engineering, mathematics, or computing is desirable.
- **GPA:** An undergraduate GPA of 2.75 or above on a 4.0 scale.
- **Prerequisites:** A specific background in computer science is required. A grade of 'B' or higher is expected in each prerequisite course.

### Application Deadlines (Priority)
- **Fall Admission:** April 1
- **Spring Admission:** October 1
- **Note:** For consideration for the Competitive Graduate Award, applications for Fall must be submitted by December 1.

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL (Paper-based):** 550
  - **TOEFL (Computer-based):** 217
  - **IELTS:** 6.5
- **Intensive English Pathway (IEP):** Successful completion of SIUE's IEP program also fulfills the English proficiency requirement.
