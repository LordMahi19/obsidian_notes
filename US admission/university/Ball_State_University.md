# Ball State University

## Admission Requirements

### Program Details
- **Program Modality:** The Master of Science in Computer Science program is offered asynchronously online.
- **Prerequisites:** The program is designed for students with or without a prior computer science background and offers foundational courses if needed.

### Admission Pathways
1.  **Standard Admission:** The traditional application process.
2.  **Performance-Based Admission:** This pathway does not require entrance exams, application fees, or initial transcripts. Full admission is granted after completing three 3-credit courses with a GPA of 3.0 or better.

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** A minimum TOEFL score of 79 or a Duolingo score of 110 is required.

### Application Deadlines
- **Fall Semester:** June 15
- **Spring Semester:** November 15

### Financials
- **Estimated Tuition:** The university estimates graduate tuition for international students at approximately **$20,898 per academic year**.
- **Financial Assistance:** The Department of Computer Science offers scholarships and graduate assistantships to outstanding applicants.
