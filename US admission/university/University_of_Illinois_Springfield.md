# University of Illinois Springfield

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **GRE:** The GRE is not required for admission to this program.

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A four-year undergraduate degree or its equivalent from an accredited university.
- **GPA:** A minimum GPA of 2.70 in the bachelor's degree.
- **Prerequisites:** Applicants must have completed the equivalent of "CSC 385 Data Structures and Algorithms" before admission.

### Application Materials
- A completed online application.
- Certified academic transcripts (with translations if necessary).
- A personal statement (250-650 words).
- A copy of your passport.

### Application Deadlines
- **Fall Admission:** May 15

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL:** 79
  - **IELTS:** 6.0
  - **Duolingo:** 105
  - **iTEP:** 3.8
  - **PTE:** 50
