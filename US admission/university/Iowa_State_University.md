# Iowa State University

## Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A 4-year Bachelor's degree or its equivalent.
- **GPA:** A minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale.

### Application Materials
- A completed online application.
- Letters of recommendation (submitted electronically).
- A Statement of Purpose (SOP).
- **GRE:** The GRE is generally required. However, MS applicants can use a free pre-application to be considered for a GRE waiver.

### Application Deadlines (MS Program)
- **Fall Admission:** June 1
- **Spring Admission:** November 1

### International Student Requirements
- **Financial Statement:** This is only required *after* an offer of admission has been made.
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 79
  - **IELTS:** 6.5
  - **PTE:** 53
  - **Duolingo:** 105
