# Indiana University Indianapolis (Formerly IUPUI)

## Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A four-year bachelor's degree or its equivalent.
- **GPA:** A minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale.
- **Prerequisites:** A strong background in computer science is expected, including:
  - Calculus & Linear Algebra
  - Statistics or Probability Theory
  - Programming & Data Structures
  - Discrete Structures or Discrete Mathematics

### Application Materials
- **Application:** A completed online application with a fee ($65-$70).
- **Letters of Recommendation:** Three letters are required.
- **Statement of Purpose:** A statement of purpose is mandatory.
- **Transcripts:** Official transcripts from all post-secondary institutions.
- **GRE:** The GRE is optional for most applicants but is strongly encouraged for those without a strong CS background. It is required for applicants who do not meet the minimum GPA requirement.

### Application Deadlines
- **Fall Semester:** April 1 (January 15 for fellowship consideration).
- **Spring Semester:** September 15.

### International Student Requirements
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 80
  - **IELTS:** 6.5
  - **Duolingo:** 110
