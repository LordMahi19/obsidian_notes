# University of North Dakota

## Admission Requirements (M.S. in Data Science)

### Academic Requirements
- **Degree:** A bachelor's degree is required.
- **GPA:** An overall undergraduate GPA of at least 3.0.

### Application Materials
- A completed online application with a fee.
- Official transcripts and a copy of your diploma or degree certificate.
- Three letters of reference.
- A Statement of Goals.
- **GRE:** The GRE General test is only required if your bachelor's degree is not from a CSAB/CSAC accredited institution.

### Application Deadlines
- **Fall Admission:** August 1
- **Spring Admission:** December 1
*(International students should confirm specific deadlines for their application process).*

### International Student Requirements
- **Financial Documentation:** A Statement of Financial Responsibility is required.
- **Passport:** A photocopy of your passport.
- **English Proficiency:** Minimum scores for admission:
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 76
  - **IELTS:** 6.0
  - **Duolingo:** 110
