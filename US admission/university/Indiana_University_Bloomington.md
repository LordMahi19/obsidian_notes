# Indiana University Bloomington

## Admission Requirements

### General Information
- **Program Duration:** 1.5 to 2 years.
- **GRE:** The GRE General Test is optional.
- **Decision Timeline:**
  - **Fall Admission:** Decisions are sent from March through late April.
  - **Spring Admission:** Decisions are sent in October.

### Application Materials
- A completed online application with a fee.
- Unofficial transcripts from all previously attended institutions.
- Three letters of recommendation.
- A current CV or resume.
- A Statement of Purpose of approximately 500 words.

### International Student Requirements
- **Degree Equivalency:** The university accepts 3-year bachelor's degrees from international students.
- **Financial Documentation:** After applying, you will be contacted by the Office of International Services (OIS) to provide financial documentation.
- **English Proficiency:** A score from TOEFL, IELTS, or Duolingo is required unless you completed your undergraduate education at a U.S. institution.
  - **TOEFL iBT:** 100
  - **IELTS:** 7.0
  - **Duolingo:** 130

### Financial Aid
- Various funding opportunities are available, including fellowships, research assistantships (RAs), and teaching assistantships (TAs).
