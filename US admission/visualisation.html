The $10k Tuition Challenge: In-State vs. International

Your goal of ~$10,000/year is a common target, but it's vital to understand the data. This figure typically represents *in-state* tuition for state residents. International students are charged *out-of-state* or *international* rates, which are significantly higher. This infographic visualizes the most affordable options based on those international rates.

Typical Funding Sources

Tuition is only part of the story. Many international students receive funding via Teaching (TA) or Research (RA) Assistantships, which often include a tuition waiver and a monthly stipend.

Beyond Tuition: Key Financials

An assistantship can make a more expensive school cheaper than a low-tuition school. Furthermore, many affordable universities are in low cost-of-living (LCOL) areas, making your stipend go further.
